About

Katy happened to be in some mysterious place. She is trying to find the way out, but meets dreadful monsters. She has to fight and defeat monsters to get a chance to come back home. Use your knowledge to win.

  • Game is made by using Javascript, Canvas and CSS-animation

  • You can check your knowledge of math, native and foreign language, geography

  • Each following level difficulty of tasks goes up
